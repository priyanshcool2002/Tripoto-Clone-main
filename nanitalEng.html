Gorgeous Cottages In Nainital That Will Leave You Awestruck

by Priya Pareek

16

232K views

Known for its incredible landscapes, lush green surroundings, scenic views and majestic mountains, Nainital is one of India’s most favorite hill stations. The beautiful lakes, forests and mountains create an idyllic vacation setting as you plan to while away your weekend in nature’s lap. The hill town not only offers nature’s beauty at its best but some really amazing properties which serve as home away from home. While there is no dearth of resorts and hotels in Nainital, cottages has their own unique charm and beauty. Staying in a cottage adds more fun to your trip. If you admire properties that are designed to reflect old world charm while offering best of amenities then here is a list of the best cottages that you can choose from.

Swiss Cottage

Colonel's Cottage

Stone Heights

The Hammock Bhimtal-Pura Stays

Taredal Cottage

Naini Cottage-Nainital

Set amid lush green surroundings overlooking majestic mountains in Nainital, Swiss Cottage is a combination of an old world charm with all luxuries. Booking your accommodation here gives you a chance to enjoy ultimate beauty, wilderness and quiet of the Himalayas. The cottage boasts of a quaint little English tea room for a perfect start to your morning. What's special: Elegant accommodation amid peaceful surrounding Cost: ₹5,600 per night Located on the Mall Road, one of the more happening places in Nainital, the Colonel's Cottage is a home-like lodging place. The accommodation offers you a chance to de-stress yourself as you laze around in the cool, mountain breeze. The cottage flaunts views of the mountains, a pretty garden dotted with colorful flowers and homely feeling. What’s special: Mountain views Cost: ₹2,500 per night Another beautiful cottage in the hill town, Hutton Cottage is where you can enjoy the sounds of nature from the surrounding oak forest. While retaining the old world charm, the cottage offers all facilities for a convenient stay. What’s special: The nearby oak forest brings nature closer to you Cost: ₹7,200 per night Stone Heights is a beautiful cottage perfect for budget travelers. The cottage is nestled in an oak forest which adds to the beauty of its surroundings. Whether you are travelling with family or your beloved, Stone Heights promises a memorable stay. What’s special: Surrounded by oak forest Cost: Starts ₹5,000 per night Beautiful independent cottages with lush grounds, the Hammock is features 15 cottages fitted with all felicities you could need. Each cottage is has its own private sit out unit overlooking the gorgeous lawn. Brimming with serenity, it's a quaint holiday stay. What’s special: Independent cottages with private sit-outs Cost: ₹6,600 per night The tranquility of Taradale Cottage and views of green valley lures travelers from across the nation. Built in British style architecture, the property features a small English garden. This quaint attic has six well-equipped rooms with modern comforts. The interiors are tastefully designed with colonial furniture, wooden ceilings and fireplaces. What’s special: Scenic views Cost: ₹6,000 per night For a homely stay book your accommodation at the Naini Cottage. Designed with utmost simplicity, the cottage has almost all the required felicities and moreover it's a home away from home.
