Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)



1. What are Mindful Retreats?
Tripoto's latest offering, Mindful Retreats are carefully curated packages that ensure a smooth travel experience away from mainstream and crowded locations. These offbeat getaways go beyond a regular weekend trip and let you experience a location deeply by engaging in immersive activities. Each retreat comes with your stay, meals and activities included in the package. Just pack and go to #FindYourCalm ...

2. How can I book?
Click the retreat you're interested in. Fill the form on the package page and make the payment to book. You can also reach out to the travel experts at Tripoto on this number: 08046162990

3.How are Mindful Retreats different from a regular holiday package?
What makes Tripoto's Mindful Retreats different from any package that you will find listed on the web are its unique range of locations and activities. We proudly curate experiences that you won't find anywhere else, no matter how many 'things to do' pages you scout through. As part of the retreat, your host (a local) will welcome you to their property and make you experience hidden gems and share secrets of the location that you won't find otherwise.

4. What do you mean by all-inclusive? What are the inclusions and exclusions?
All-inclusive means that your stay, meals and activities mentioned under 'inclusions' on the page of each retreat. Exclusions are transportation and anything not mentioned in Inclusions.

5. Are there any discounts available?
No. We curate mindful, happy and meaningful holidays, and we do it at the best price possible! Instead of offering deals, we believe in getting you the most memorable and quality holiday options.

6. Is transportation included?
No, but can be arranged on special request if you need it.
br 7. Do these retreats have fixed departure or can I choose my own date?
We have an open calendar, you are free to choose a date of your convenience to experience any retreat.

8. Are these retreats suitable for female solo travellers? How are you ensuring safety?
Yes! Safety of female travellers (solo or otherwise) is our biggest priority. Each property goes through several rounds of check before being onboarded.

9. Can I take my parents (senior citizens) or kid on these retreats?
Yes. There could be a few activities as part of the retreat that may not be suitable for kids or senior citizens. But, all locations are accessible and safe. You can confirm on a case-by-case basis before booking.

10. What is the flexible booking option? How can I avail it?
Each retreat comes with a free one-time rescheduling option. Let us know 72 hours ahead of your check-in date and pick another date as per your convenience within the next three months.

11. Can we use Tripoto Credits to book these retreats?
Yes. You can avail 10% off using Tripoto Credits, if you have collected enough Credits by creating content on Tripoto.
